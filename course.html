FAQs
Can I just enroll in a single course? I'm not interested in the entire Specializat

Yes, you can enroll in a single course. Each course is designed to provide in-depth knowledge on specific topics. If you're interested in only one area, you can opt for that course without taking the entire specialization. However, completing all courses in a specialization can provide you with a comprehensive understanding of the subject.

What is the refund policy?

Our refund policy allows for a full refund within 14 days of enrollment, provided you haven’t accessed more than 25% of the course content. After this period or if you have accessed significant content, refunds are not available. Please refer to our detailed refund policy for more specific information.

What background knowledge is necessary

Background knowledge varies by course. For beginner-level courses, no prior knowledge is typically required, while intermediate and advanced courses may require foundational skills in the subject. Each course page includes specific prerequisites to help you assess whether the course is suitable for you.

Do i need to take the courses in a specific ord

If you're pursuing a specialization, it’s recommended to follow the sequence provided, as each course builds on the previous one. For individual courses, you can take them in any order based on your interest and background knowledge. Course details will specify any recommended progression.
